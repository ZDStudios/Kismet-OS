# Zorin direction within Kismet OS

The strongest Zorin OS lessons for Kismet OS are not about copying assets. They are about product behavior.

## What to emulate
- polished first-run experience
- coherence across login, desktop, boot, and defaults
- premium feel without clutter
- approachable desktop setup for people who still want power underneath

## What not to copy blindly
- brand identity
- direct visual cloning
- unnecessary restrictions on power-user flexibility

## Kismet interpretation
Kismet OS should feel like:
- Ubuntu practicality
- Zorin-grade coherence
- KDE flexibility
- selected Garuda-style confidence where it genuinely helps
