# Brand notes

## Tone
- clean
- sharp
- modern
- technical without sounding sterile

## Avoid
- generic cyberpunk buzzword soup
- exaggerated AI claims
- overdone gradients everywhere
- copy that reads like it was generated in one breath and never edited

## Direction
Kismet OS should feel premium, restrained, and confident. The best reference point is a product page written by someone who likes both Linux and good design, not someone trying to summon venture capital with neon smoke.
